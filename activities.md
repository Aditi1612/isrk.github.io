In 2024–2025, ISRK organized numerous offline events across Seoul, Gwangju, Busan, Daegu, Seongnam, and other cities, as well as online events focused on education and student affairs.

---

## Educational Programs & Student Affairs

<div class="activity-section">

### Scholarship & Research Webinars
- **Online Webinar on Scholarship Programs** — BS, MS, Ph.D. and Postdoc opportunities in Korea
- **ISRK–ACS Webinar** — Tips for Scholarly Publishing (collaboration with American Chemical Society)
- **KLiK JobKorea–ISRK Online Job Fair** — Connecting members with employers in Korea

### Academic Collaboration
- **India/Korea University Collaboration & MoU Workshops** — Facilitating research partnerships
- **Invited Talks** — Scientists from academia and industry sharing expertise
- **Networking Events** — Job and research opportunity information sessions
- **Academic Guidance Cell** — Peer support and mentorship for students

### Student Affairs
- **Student Meetups** — Regular community gatherings for new and existing students
- **ISRK–Embassy of India Orientation & Networking Event** — Welcoming new arrivals to Korea
- **ISRK Student Affairs Meetings** — Regular coordination and support sessions

</div>

## Cultural Programs & Events

<div class="activity-section cultural">

### Major Festivals
1. **Foundation Day & Networking Event** — Annual celebration on May 8th, Seoul *(Collaboration: JobBridge Korea)*
2. **Indo-Utsav (Independence Day)** — Celebrating India's Independence, Busan *(Collaboration: India Center, Busan)*
3. **Garba Mahotsav** — Traditional dance festival, Daegu
4. **Deepotsav (Diwali)** — Festival of lights, Seoul
5. **Holi — Holi Hai** — Festival of colors, Chuncheon *(in collaboration with IIK)*
6. **Bollywood Night** — Music and dance celebration, Seoul
7. **Eid Milan** — Community Eid gathering, Gunpo (Suwon)
8. **Year-End Party** — Annual community year-end celebration, Daegu

### Cultural Exchange
- **SPIC MACAY — Lycée français de Séoul** — Classical Odissi lecture demonstration, Seoul
- **Seoul Friendship Festival** — International cultural exchange
- **Sinchon International Youth Festival** — Youth cultural participation
- **Yoga Day** — Celebrating India's cultural wellness tradition
- **NIIED Year-End Party** — Collaboration with National Institute for International Education

### Media & Creative Programs
- **Travel & Food Vlogs** — Exploring Korean destinations and introducing Korean cuisine
- **Instagram Content** — Visual storytelling from the community
- **Podcast: Life in Korea, Work & Experiences** — Multilingual podcast in English and regional languages featuring guests from academia and industry (Walk-the-Talk Show)

</div>

## Sports & Outdoor Activities

<div class="activity-section sports">

### Team Sports
- **Cricket Championship** — Annual ISRK Cricket Tournament, GIST, Gwangju
- **Football** — Friendly matches and mini-leagues
- **Cycling** — Seoul and surrounding areas

### Outdoor Adventures
- **Hiking / Trekking** — Regular hikes across Korea
- **Adventure Expedition** — Organized hiking across **16 cities in Korea** in 2023: Seoul, Suwon, Incheon, Daejeon, Daegu, Busan, Ulsan, Gwangju, Jinju, Geoje, Cheonan, Jeju, and more
- **Tours & Trips** — Organized with Social Engagement and Student Affairs teams
- **Temple Stay, Gwangju** — Cultural immersion experience
- **Boseong Tea Garden** — Scenic trip to Korea's famous tea fields
- **Goheung Yuja Festival** — Local Korean festival participation
- **Suncheon Garden** — Nature and garden exploration

</div>
