ISRK organizes a wide range of activities throughout the year to support the holistic development of the Indian student and researcher community in Korea. Our programs span three major areas:

---

## Educational Activities

<div class="activity-section educational">

### Workshops & Seminars
- **Research Methodology Workshops** — Guidance on writing papers, grant applications, and academic publishing
- **Korean Language Classes** — Beginner to intermediate Korean language sessions
- **Career Development Webinars** — Resume building, interview skills, and career planning
- **Scholarship Information Sessions** — Information on KGSP, NRF, BK21 and other funding opportunities

### Academic Support
- **Mentorship Program** — Pairing new students with experienced researchers for guidance
- **Study Groups** — Subject-specific study circles across universities
- **Lab/Research Collaboration** — Facilitating inter-university research partnerships
- **University Orientation** — Helping new Indian arrivals understand the Korean academic system

### Guest Lectures
ISRK regularly invites distinguished professors, industry professionals, and government officials to share knowledge and expertise with the community.

</div>

---

## Cultural Activities

<div class="activity-section cultural">

### Festivals & Celebrations
- **Diwali Celebration** — Annual festival of lights celebrated with the broader Korean community
- **Holi** — The festival of colors, bringing vibrancy and joy to Korea
- **Independence Day** — Celebration of India's Independence on August 15th
- **Republic Day** — Commemorating India's Republic Day on January 26th
- **Onam, Dussehra, Eid** — Celebrating the diverse festivals of India

### Cultural Exchange
- **India-Korea Cultural Night** — Showcasing Indian music, dance, and cuisine to Korean friends
- **Classical Music & Dance Performances** — In collaboration with SPIC MACAY
- **Indian Food Festival** — Celebrating India's rich culinary traditions
- **Traditional Attire Day** — Wearing India's diverse regional traditional clothes

### Arts & Creativity
- **Photography Contests** — Capturing life in Korea through an Indian lens
- **Essay & Art Competitions** — Expressing the experience of being Indian in Korea
- **Cultural Documentaries** — Sharing stories of the Indian community in Korea

</div>

## Sports Activities

<div class="activity-section sports">

### Team Sports
- **Cricket Tournament** — ISRK annual cricket league across Korean cities
- **Football (Soccer) Matches** — Friendly matches and mini-leagues
- **Volleyball** — Team tournaments and recreational games
- **Badminton** — Doubles and singles tournaments

### Individual Sports
- **Table Tennis** — Individual and team competitions
- **Chess Tournament** — Online and in-person chess championships
- **Athletics** — Track and field events during sports meets

### Annual Sports Meet
ISRK hosts an **Annual Sports Day** that brings together students from universities across South Korea for a full day of competitive and recreational sporting events.

</div>
