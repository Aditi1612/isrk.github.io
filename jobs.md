## Research Opportunities

### Postdoctoral Positions
Korea offers numerous postdoctoral positions funded by top research bodies. Key funding sources include:

- **NRF (National Research Foundation of Korea)** — Postdoctoral fellowships in all disciplines
- **BK21 (Brain Korea 21)** — Research positions at leading Korean universities
- **IBS (Institute for Basic Science)** — Research positions at cutting-edge research centers
- **KAIST, POSTECH, SNU** — Direct postdoc openings at top Korean universities

### PhD Scholarships
- **KGSP (Korean Government Scholarship Program)** — Full scholarship for international students including tuition, living expenses, and Korean language training
- **University-funded PhD positions** — Many Korean universities offer fully funded PhD programs
- **Industry-Academia Research Programs** — Joint positions between companies and universities

### Research Internships
- **Summer/Winter Research Internships** — Available at KAIST, POSTECH, Yonsei, Korea University, and others
- **Government Research Institutes** — KIST, ETRI, KERI, KRISS, and other national labs regularly hire international interns

## Job Opportunities in Korea

### Industries Hiring International Talent
- **Technology & IT** — Samsung, LG, SK, Kakao, Naver, Hyundai
- **Manufacturing & Engineering** — Automotive, semiconductors, display technology
- **Pharmaceuticals & Biotech** — A growing sector with international openings
- **Finance & Consulting** — International firms with Korean operations
- **Education** — Teaching positions and educational content roles

### Job Platforms in Korea
- **[Korea Jobs](https://www.koreajobbriefing.com)** — Job listings for international professionals
- **[WorknPlay](https://www.worknplay.co.kr)** — English-language jobs in Korea
- **[Jobkorea](https://www.jobkorea.co.kr)** — Korea's major job portal
- **[LinkedIn Korea](https://www.linkedin.com)** — International job listings including Korea
- **ISRK WhatsApp Groups** — Community job sharing in our member groups
