## Executive Committee

ISRK is led by a dedicated executive committee of volunteers who are committed to serving the Indian student and researcher community in Korea. Our team includes representatives from universities across the country.

## University Representatives

ISRK has **100+ student representatives** from over **70 universities** across South Korea. These representatives serve as the local point of contact for ISRK members at their respective universities.

<div class="info-box">

</div>

## Join the Team

ISRK runs entirely on the passion and dedication of volunteers. If you want to contribute to the community and join our team, we welcome applications for:

- Executive committee positions (elected annually)
- Department volunteer roles
- Event organizing committees
- University representative positions

**Contact us:** [isrk.association@gmail.com](mailto:isrk.association@gmail.com)
