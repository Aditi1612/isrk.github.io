## Who We Are

ISRK (Indian Students and Researchers in Korea) is a non-profit voluntary organization that supports Indian-origin students and researchers in South Korea. Founded in **May 2019**, ISRK was created with a single vision — to bring all Indian students and researchers in Korea under one platform, promoting unity, supporting academic and professional growth, and helping members adjust to life in Korea.

Today, ISRK has grown to over **5,000 members** and includes **100+ Indian student representatives** from more than **70 universities** across South Korea.

---

## Our Mission

ISRK aims to connect all Indian-origin students and researchers residing in South Korea and provide them with:

- **Educational & Research Support** — academic guidance, scholarships, and research collaboration
- **Professional Networking** — job opportunities and career development
- **Social & Recreational Activities** — cultural events, sports, and community gatherings
- **Community Assistance** — helping new arrivals settle and adjust to life in Korea

## Our Partners & MoU Signatories

ISRK has signed Memoranda of Understanding (MoU) with several prestigious organizations:

- **RIST** — Research Center of Indian Culture on Science and Technology
- **UCC** — Universal Cultural Center
- **SPIC MACAY** — Society for the Promotion of Indian Classical Music and Culture Amongst Youth
- **SENTBE Remittance**
- **Chakra Indian Restaurant**
- **KOINA** — Korea-India Association
- **ACN** — Asian Community News Network
- **HCGKR** — Honorary Consulate General of The Republic of Korea in Hyderabad
